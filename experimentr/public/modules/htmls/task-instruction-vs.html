Visual Search Task Instruction

In this task, you will be asked to find a set of target edges in each trial. You will be asked to find all edges with either a given strength level or a given certainty level.

In each trial, You will see a network graph, a legend for strength levels and certainty levels, and the characteristics of the targets. You can click on an edge to select it as a target. A selected edge will become red. You can click on a selected edge to cancel the selection.

There are in total 120 trials for this task. You will have 15 seconds to work on each trial. You can take breaks between trials. There will also be a designated break period after each group of 30 trials.
